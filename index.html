Who Is Jay Smith

The Man Behind the Machines

In the world of enterprise infrastructure, where uptime is everything and downtime is unforgivable, one name rises above the humming racks and blinking LEDs: Jay Smith, Vice President of Data Center Operations and Engineering at Evocative.

With over 25 years of experience in telecommunications and data center management, Jay is the co-founder of Cyberverse -- one of the most trusted Internet infrastructure companies in the industry. Today, he's responsible for all Evocative data center operations across the United States.
